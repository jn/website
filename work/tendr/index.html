Tendr - "No one likes being hungry."

McHacks Winner 2015 | View Entry

You don't have any food in your fridge, and you can't decide what to eat. You're on the verge of scurvy, so Kraft Dinner for the sixth time in a row is out of the question.

You get a message from a match on Tinder. You want to reply with a smooth pick up line, but you're too hungry to think of one. Then it dawns on you... don't you wish there were a Tinder for food?

Welcome to Tendr.

Tendr matches you with delicious meals available in your local area. With Tendr, you can finally find the dinner you truly desire.

Being hungry is hard. Tendr is easy.
